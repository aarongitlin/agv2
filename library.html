My Library
I decided to document a few thoughts on books I've read recently, especially those pertinent to my profession. While this is primarily for my own education and memory, I thought it could maybe be useful to others as well.
Just Enough Research
Erika Hall
A short, simple summary of some basic UX research principles. Its main argument is that "research" is really just critical thinking by another name, and that projects of any size benefit from at least some research.
The Beginning of Infinity: Explanations that transform the world
David Deutsch
Experience Required: How to become a UX leader regardless of your role
Robert Hoekman Jr
Graphesis: Visual Forms of Knowledge Production
Johanna Drucker
UX Strategy: How to Devise Innovative Digital Products that People Want
Jaime Levy
Designing with Data: Improving the User Experience with A/B Testing
Rochelle King, Elizabeth Churchill, Caitlin Tan
The Design Way: Intentional Change in an Unpredictable World
Harold G. Nelson, Erik Stolterman
